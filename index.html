Archive of Conferences (AoC) is a platform created to publish the research papers of the conferences organized worldwide. We are providing the publication support to the conference organizers in order to make the research available online. 

AIC platform works for the collaboration between the Academic and Scientific Institutions to publish the conference proceedings. We are also providing the services for indexing of the papers in reputed databases.

We invite the conference organizers all over the world to publish the special issues of the State, National and International Conferences, Symposium, Events.
